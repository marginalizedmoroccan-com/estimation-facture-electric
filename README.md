# Astro App: Estimation Facture Électricité au Maroc

## why i made this app

I made this app because I wanted to have a simple and easy-to-use tool to estimate my electricity bill in Morocco. I also wanted to have a place where I could store my electricity bills and track my electricity consumption over time.

## how to use this app

1. Go to the website: https://electricite.marginalizedmoroccan.com
2. Enter your electricity consumption in kWh
3. Click on the "Calculate" button
4. The app will calculate your electricity bill and display it on the screen
5. You can also see your electricity consumption history and track your electricity consumption over time
